⚠ Safety Requirements — Read Before Starting

A. Removal of the steering rack assembly can be hazardous. Get instructor assistance if you are in doubt about tool operation or procedure.

B. Wear eye protection at all times while working under the vehicle.

C. Engage safety locks on the hoist before working under the vehicle.

D. Relieve power steering fluid pressure before disconnecting any hydraulic lines. Wipe up all fluid spills immediately — power steering fluid is a slip and fire hazard.

Part / Component

Why Replace / Inspect
Rack mounting bushings / grommets Compress and harden over time; already removed — cost effective to replace
Tie-rod end locknuts Single-use torque-to-yield fasteners; must not be reused
Rack boots (bellows) Inspect for cracks, tears, grease loss — replace if any defect found
Hydraulic line sealing washers / O-rings Crush seals deform upon removal; always replace to prevent fluid leaks
Pinion shaft grommet / seal Prevents moisture intrusion; already accessible — inspect and replace if deteriorated
