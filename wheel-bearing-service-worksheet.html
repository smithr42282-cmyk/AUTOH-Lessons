Section 4 — Magnetic Sensor Test Card — New Bearing Pre-Install Check

⚠ MANDATORY — Do Not Skip

This section must be completed BEFORE pressing the new bearing into the knuckle. A bearing that fails this check must be returned. Do not install a bearing that fails the magnetic sensor card test.

Step Verification Task Result / Observation Pass / Fail
1 Inspect test card — flat, undamaged, not demagnetized
2 Locate encoder ring on new bearing — confirm side of bearing it is on
3 Hold card flush against encoder ring — observe stripe pattern visibility
4 Rotate bearing slowly — confirm stripe pattern is consistent through full rotation
5 Confirm encoder ring orientation for install — ring will face ABS sensor after installation
