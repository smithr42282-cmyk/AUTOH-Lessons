⚠ Safety Notes — Read Before Starting
⚠ Critical Safety Requirements

A. Compressing the coil spring is extremely dangerous. The stored spring energy can cause severe injury if released suddenly. Get instructor assistance if you are in doubt about the operation of tools or procedure.

B. Wear eye protection at all times during disassembly and reassembly.

C. Use safety locks on the hoist whenever the vehicle is lifted.
