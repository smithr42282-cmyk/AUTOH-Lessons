Section 2 — Safety
⚠ Critical Safety Requirements — Read Before Proceeding

A. Compressing the coil spring stores extreme amounts of energy and is very dangerous. Get instructor assistance before using the spring compressor if you are unsure of the procedure.

B. Wear eye protection at all times during disassembly and reassembly.

C. Always engage safety locks on the hoist before working under the vehicle.

Q3
Step 3 — Safety Check

In your own words, explain why spring compression is the most dangerous step of strut service. What can happen if the spring releases suddenly?

Tip: Think about the amount of force stored in a compressed coil spring and what direction that energy would release if the compressor slipped or failed.
