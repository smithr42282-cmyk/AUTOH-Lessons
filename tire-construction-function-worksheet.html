G2 — Plug-Patch Repair: Order the Steps (1–7)

Buff and clean the repair area from the inside
Remove the tire from the rim and inspect internally
Apply vulcanizing cement to the buffed area
Re-mount, inflate to spec, check for leaks with soapy water
Mark the puncture location and remove the object
Install the combination plug-patch from the inside
Ream the puncture channel to create a uniform hole
